# Quick Screen Workflow

Use this workflow for a fast initial read before a full council evaluation.

## Purpose

Identify whether an idea deserves deeper evaluation, immediate rejection, or targeted validation.

## Participating Agents

- Council Director
- Market Research Lead
- Growth & Distribution Strategist
- Financial Realism Advisor
- Contrarian Critic

## Output

- One-paragraph summary
- Top three reasons to continue
- Top three reasons to stop
- Critical assumption
- Fastest useful validation test
- Provisional vote from each participating agent

## Constraint

This is not a full verdict. It is a triage step.
