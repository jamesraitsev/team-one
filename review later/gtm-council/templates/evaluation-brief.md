# Evaluation Brief Template

Use this template to brief GTM Council.

## Opportunity

Describe the idea, product, company, or strategic move.

## Target Customer

Who is the buyer? Who is the user?

## Problem

What pain or job is being addressed?

## Current Alternatives

What do customers use or do today?

## Proposed Solution

What is the product or service?

## Business Model

How will it make money?

## Distribution Hypothesis

How will customers find, try, and buy it?

## Evidence Already Available

List customer conversations, usage data, revenue, competitors, market signals, or other proof.

## Constraints

Team, capital, timeline, technical, regulatory, operational, or distribution constraints.

## Decision Needed

What decision should the council help make?
