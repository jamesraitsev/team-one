# Standard Evaluation Framework

Use this framework for full GTM Council evaluations.

## 1. Executive Summary

- Bottom-line recommendation
- Highest-conviction reasons
- Most important unresolved disagreement
- Next decision point

## 2. Problem & Customer

- Target customer
- Pain severity
- Current alternatives
- Switching friction
- Evidence strength

## 3. Market & Competition

- Market category
- Direct competitors
- Indirect competitors
- Non-consumption and workarounds
- Incumbent risk

## 4. Positioning & Moat

- Positioning statement
- Differentiation
- Defensibility
- Copy risk
- Compounding advantage, if any

## 5. Distribution & Monetization

- Likely acquisition channels
- Sales motion
- CAC realism
- Pricing model
- Margin and payback risks

## 6. Product & Technical Feasibility

- MVP scope
- First-use value
- Retention mechanism
- Technical dependencies
- Operational requirements

## 7. Operational Risks

- Support burden
- Compliance and security
- Data quality and access
- Implementation complexity
- Team capacity constraints

## 8. Why This Wins or Fails

- Most credible win condition
- Most credible failure mode
- Critical assumptions
- What would change the recommendation

## 9. MVP & Unknowns

- Smallest useful validation test
- Required evidence
- Fastest disconfirming test
- Unknowns to resolve before scaling

## 10. Assumption Register

- Top decision-controlling assumptions
- Owner agent for each assumption
- Current evidence status
- Confirming evidence needed
- Disconfirming evidence or kill criteria

## 11. Disagreement Ledger

- Material disagreements
- Agents involved
- Whether disagreement is about evidence, interpretation, weighting, or risk tolerance
- What would resolve the disagreement
- Minority view to preserve

## 12. Validation Roadmap

- Priority tests
- Owner for each test
- Purpose
- Strong confirming signal
- Strong disconfirming signal

## 13. Voting Results

- Vote table by agent
- Conviction
- Primary concern
- Critical assumption
- Minority opinions

## 14. Final Verdict

- Recommendation
- Conditions for pursuit
- Conditions for rejection
- Suggested next action
- Decision-controlling assumption

## 15. Confidence

- Confidence level
- Evidence gaps
- Assumption sensitivity
- Where the council may be wrong
