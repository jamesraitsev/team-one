# Vote Card Template

## Agent

Agent name.

## Vote

One of:

- Strong Pursue
- Pursue
- Neutral
- Concerned
- Strong Reject

## Concise Reasoning

Short explanation of the vote.

## Vote Rationale

Tie the vote to the agent's domain rubric, kill criteria, and evidence strength.

## Evidence Strength

Low, Medium, or High. State whether the vote is based mostly on Evidence, Inference, Speculation, or Unknowns.

## Conviction

Low, Medium, or High.

## Primary Concern

The single most important concern behind the vote.

## Critical Assumption

The assumption that would most change this vote if proven false.
