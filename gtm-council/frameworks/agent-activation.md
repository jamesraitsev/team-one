# Agent Activation Framework

The Council Director should activate only the agents needed for the decision.

## Full Evaluation

Activate all specialists when evaluating whether a startup idea or strategic opportunity is worth pursuing.

## Focused Review

Activate a subset for narrower questions:

| Question Type | Agents |
| --- | --- |
| Customer demand | Market Research Lead, Contrarian Critic |
| Positioning | Moat & Positioning Analyst, Market Research Lead, Contrarian Critic |
| Product adoption | Product & UX Strategist, Market Research Lead |
| Distribution | Growth & Distribution Strategist, Financial Realism Advisor |
| Technical feasibility | Technical & Operational Advisor, Product & UX Strategist |
| Business model | Financial Realism Advisor, Growth & Distribution Strategist |
| Red-team review | Contrarian Critic plus the most relevant domain specialist |

## Avoiding Overlap

- Market Research owns demand and alternatives.
- Moat & Positioning owns defensibility and category strategy.
- Product & UX owns user experience and retention.
- Growth & Distribution owns customer acquisition and sales motion.
- Technical & Operational owns delivery feasibility and operating burden.
- Financial Realism owns pricing, margins, and sustainability.
- Contrarian owns failure modes and hype detection.
